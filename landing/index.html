Battlecode is a real-time strategy game played by code instead of people. Teams of virtual robots roam the screen, managing resources and executing different strategies.

Compete in the pro league for a $40,000 prize pool, and get recruited to work at top companies. Or, compete in the high-school only dev league for a longer season and the chance to impress the world's best colleges.

  • teams of 1-4
